日复一日
Day by Day


你知道吗？日子好像每天都一样，但是我很幸福。
You know what? Every day seems similar, but I'm happiness.



在英国，我跟这边我最好的朋友住在一块，我们一起看电视，我们一起做饭，我们谈心，我们打闹。
In England, I live with my best friend here, we watch TV together, we cook together, we talk, we play.
在中国，完整美满的家庭，我很幸运，他们可以一直爱我，我也爱他们，互相陪伴。
In China, complete and happy family, I am very lucky, they can always love me, I love them, accompany each other.
我离开家第七年了，我最珍惜在家的时光。我怕有天真的抓不住了。
It's been seven years since I left home, and I cherish my time at home the most. I'm afraid I'm gonna lose my grip.

我很喜欢过平淡的生活，不喜欢大起大落，只喜欢细水长流。
I like to live a plain life, do not like big ups and downs, only like a long stream.

我上学，我生活。我解决自己的文化问题和温饱问题。
I go to college, I live. I solve my own study and food problems.

好多人都问我：“找对象了吗？没谈男朋友啊？” 我自己努力的生活挺好的，我这个J人很怕因为外界打乱一切。
A lot of people ask me: "Have you found a partner? No boyfriend?" I make a good life on my own, and I'm a "J" (decided by MBTI test) person who's afraid of messing everything up because of the outside world.
我会很封闭吗？我觉得我总有一天会拾起感情的。
Am I be closed off? I think I'm gonna get back into it one day.

我爱听歌，在家听，出门听，悲伤听，快乐也听，甚至我喜欢在静不下心的时候听。
I love listening to music, at home, out of the house, sad listen, happy listen, even I like to listen when I can not calm down.
喜欢沉浸在各种类型的歌曲里，每次都仿佛到了另一个世界，我会深思歌词，我爱找共鸣的感觉。
I like to immerse myself in various types of songs, every time it seems to be another world, I will think deeply about the lyrics, I love to find the feeling of resonance.
我的每一天都需要歌，所以我把它写在这一篇章。
I need a song every day, so I wrote it in this chapter.

我期待我的每一天，可能是我认为每天都会长大，都会进步，都会过的更好吧。
I look forward to my every day, maybe I think every day will grow up, will progress, will be better.
其实我是从悲观主义者变过来的，某一天就突然想开了，我需要让自己正面生活。
In fact, I turned from a pessimist, one day suddenly want to open, I need to let myself live a positive life.


所以明天见吧，世界！🌎🌍🌏
So see you tomorrow, world!
